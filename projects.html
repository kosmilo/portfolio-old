Upside Down

Upside Down is a simple platformer made in Unity. In the game the player has to solve puzzles and touch the green platform to get to the next level. It also has cubes that player can push around and two other kinds of platforms; a blue one that switches the gravity, and a pink one that makes the corresponding platform or wall disapear.
